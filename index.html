Vestique AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2026

Vestique AI is an AI-powered fashion, outfit recommendation, virtual try-on, and social styling platform. Your privacy and data security are important to us.

1. Information We Collect

Vestique AI may collect the following information:

  • Name, username, email address, and profile information
  • Uploaded clothing photos and wardrobe images
  • Outfit preferences, style selections, and fashion interests
  • User-generated content such as posts, comments, and shared outfits
  • Virtual try-on photos uploaded by users
  • Subscription and premium membership information
  • Device information, analytics, and app usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Generate AI outfit recommendations
  • Provide virtual try-on experiences
  • Improve personalized fashion suggestions
  • Enable wardrobe management features
  • Support social features including profiles, posts, and interactions
  • Improve app performance, security, and user experience
  • Manage subscriptions and premium services

3. Photos and Uploaded Content

Users may upload clothing photos, profile photos, and virtual try-on images. These images are used only for app functionality and personalization features.

4. Authentication and Accounts

Vestique AI may support email/password login, Google Sign-In, and account-based features. Users are responsible for protecting their login credentials.

5. Premium Subscriptions

Some features in Vestique AI require a premium subscription. Payments are securely processed by Google Play. Vestique AI does not directly store payment card information.

6. Third-Party Services

Vestique AI may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Analytics and AI-related technologies

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when necessary to operate the application, improve services, comply with legal obligations, or protect users and the platform.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user information. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security.

9. Children's Privacy

Vestique AI is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
