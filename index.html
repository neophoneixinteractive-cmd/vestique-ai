10. Child Safety Standards

Vestique AI strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content on the platform.

We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and take appropriate measures to detect, prevent, and report abusive or exploitative content involving minors.

Our safety measures may include:

  • Content moderation and monitoring
  • User reporting and blocking systems
  • Immediate removal of prohibited content
  • Cooperation with law enforcement where required by law

Users who violate these policies may have their accounts suspended or permanently removed.
